About This Project

Welcome to our platform! This project is part of my second-year practicum where I'm learning FastAPI and practicing JavaScript Fetch. The goal is to create a cool place for students at my university. Here, they can do lots of things like sell their stuff after they finish school, talk to each other, share cool stuff, and know what's happening around campus.

Objective

Our main aim is to make a website that's super useful for students. We want it to be easy to use and have lots of cool stuff to do. We hope it helps students feel more connected and makes life a bit easier for them.

Key Features

Student Interaction: On our website, students can chat, join forums, and make friends. It's like a cool hangout spot just for students!

Marketplace: After graduation, students often have things they don't need anymore. Our website lets them sell these things to other students. It's a win-win!

Content Sharing: Students can share all kinds of stuff on our website, like class notes, art, or interesting articles. It's a great way to show off talents and help each other out.

Stay Updated: We make sure students know what's happening at the university. Our website has news, event calendars, and notifications to keep everyone in the loop.

Vision

We want our website to be the go-to place for students. We're always working to make it better and add more cool stuff. Our goal is to make university life more fun and connected for everyone.

Thanks for being part of our journey! Let's make university life awesome together!
